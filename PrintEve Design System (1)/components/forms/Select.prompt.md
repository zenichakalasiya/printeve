**Select** — restyled native `<select>` with custom chevron and the standard field focus ring.

```jsx
<Select label="Paper finish" placeholder="Choose finish"
  options={["Matte", "Gloss", "Soft-touch", "Recycled"]} />
<Select label="Quantity" options={[{value:"100",label:"100 pcs"},{value:"250",label:"250 pcs"}]} />
```
