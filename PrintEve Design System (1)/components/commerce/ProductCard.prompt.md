**ProductCard** — featured-product showcase with image, price, rating, corner badge and a hover-revealed View CTA. Composes `Badge` + `Rating`.

```jsx
<ProductCard name="Premium Business Cards" price="₹499" rating={4.8} reviews={1240}
  badge="Bestseller" imageSrc="assets/illustrations/business-cards.svg"
  href="/products/business-cards" />
```
