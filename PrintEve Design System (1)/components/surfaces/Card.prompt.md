**Card** — rounded (12px), softly-shadowed surface. Compose with `CardHeader`, `CardBody`, `CardFooter` or pass `padded`.

```jsx
<Card hoverable>
  <CardHeader title="Premium Print Quality" description="Offset & digital on pro stocks." />
  <CardBody>…</CardBody>
  <CardFooter><Button>Learn more</Button></CardFooter>
</Card>
```

`hoverable` lifts the card with a green border on hover; `flat` removes the shadow.
